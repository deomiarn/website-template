## TASK

Create sitemap and SEO strategy with keyword research.

### 1. Keyword Research (WebSearch)

Use WebSearch to find:
- Competitor keywords (search "[business type] [location]")
- Search volume indicators
- Related search terms
- Long-tail opportunities

### 2. Competitor Analysis

Analyze top 3 competitors:
- What keywords do they target?
- What content structure do they use?
- What are their weaknesses?

### 3. Output Files

Create:
- **docs/sitemap.md** - Pages, sections, priorities
- **docs/seo-strategy.md** - Keywords per page with ranking targets

### Rules

- Impressum and Datenschutz mandatory (excluded from page count)
- All sections from shadcnblocks.com
- Bilingual support (DE/EN)
- Local SEO pages only when explicitly requested
- Each page needs: primary keyword, secondary keywords, ranking target
