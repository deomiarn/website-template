# Blog Template

## Global Sections
- Header (Navbar)
- Footer

## Core Sections (Required)
1. Hero - Blog purpose/topics overview
2. Blog - Article listings (featured/recent)
3. Blog - Category/tag filters
4. CTA - Newsletter subscription

## Optional Sections
- Stats - Content metrics (articles published, readers)
- Resources - Downloadable content
- Testimonial - Reader/subscriber feedback

## Conversion Goal
Primary: Newsletter subscription, article engagement
Secondary: Product awareness, thought leadership

## User Journey
From: Organic search, social media, homepage
To: Individual blog posts, newsletter signup, product pages
