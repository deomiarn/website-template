# About Template

## Global Sections
- Header (Navbar)
- Footer

## Core Sections (Required)
1. Hero - Mission/vision statement
2. Stats - Company milestones
3. Timeline - Company history/journey
4. Team - Key people/leadership
5. Awards - Credibility markers
6. Testimonial - Customer validation
7. CTA - Join team or contact

## Optional Sections
- About - Extended story/values
- Logos - Partners/clients
- Gallery - Office/culture photos

## Conversion Goal
Primary: Build trust, humanize brand
Secondary: Recruit talent, contact inquiries

## User Journey
From: Homepage, job listings, media coverage
To: Careers, contact, services
