# Blog Post Template

## Global Sections
- Header (Navbar)
- Footer

## Core Sections (Required)
1. Hero - Title, author, date, reading time
2. Blogpost - Article content (structured)
3. CTA - Newsletter subscription
4. Blog - Related posts

## Optional Sections
- Testimonial - Social proof inline
- Resources - Related downloads/guides
- Contact - Expert consultation offer

## Conversion Goal
Primary: Content engagement, newsletter signup
Secondary: Product interest, social sharing

## User Journey
From: Organic search, blog index, social media
To: Related posts, newsletter, product pages
